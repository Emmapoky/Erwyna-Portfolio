About

A little
about me.

I'm an Advanced Computer Science student at Monash University Malaysia, focused on AI and software development — with hands-on experience building LLM-powered web apps, ML prototypes, and analytics dashboards.

Recent work spans a ReactJS chatbot integrating Gemini, OpenAI, and MLC-LLM at NTT DATA, an AI-powered learning platform built at Gamuda AI Academy, and a Top 10 finalist placement at the L'Oréal x Monash Datathon 2025. I like translating technical metrics into stories non-technical stakeholders can act on.

When I'm not shipping code, I'm usually somewhere in Blender, Photoshop, or reading about the next thing to build.

Toxic Atmosphere

Java · Design principles

A modular atmosphere system for a text-based survival game. Built around an AtmosphericServicesFactory that wires a data parser, corruptors (hazards, economy disruption, undead spawning), and an API client — each dependent only on interfaces. Extensible by design: the scanner never needs to know which specific effects exist.

✦ 04 · ETW2001 · Business Analytics

Superstore Strategy Dashboard

Data viz · Macroeconomic overlay

A dashboard investigating what drives Superstore's business performance and where hidden profit risks live. Surfaces a severe discount-loss trap (markdowns over 20% actively erode margin), then overlays internal sales against standardized World Bank and OECD leading indicators to show the macro correlation. Includes a raw-data tab so users can cross-check every insight.
